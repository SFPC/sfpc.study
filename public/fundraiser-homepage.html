Donate today!

An open-hearted effort towards mutual aid and reciprocity

SFPC’s first ever Community Fundraiser is an open-hearted effort to build our school on a foundation of mutual aid and reciprocity. As we reject the old financial models of private education, we’re looking to create something new, beautiful, and expansive in its place. We aim to create a learning environment where no one is ever turned away due to their inability to pay tuition. To bring this vision to life, we ask our community members with access to high salaries, generational wealth, and savings to donate generously. We are deeply grateful for every contribution.
